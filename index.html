NEXUS Protocol

Information Finance (InfoFi)

1. Concept

NEXUS turns information velocity into a tradable asset class. We define InfoFi as the financialization of attention, sentiment, and data veracity.

2. Prediction Markets

Users take Long (Agree) or Short (Disagree) positions on binary outcomes derived from real-time social and on-chain data streams.

  • Dynamic Odds: Adjusted by AI based on market velocity.
  • Settlement: Automated via decentralized data oracles.

3. The AI House

NEXUS utilizes a simulated "AI House" model for liquidity, managing risk dynamically across all active markets.

4. Roadmap

  • Phase 1 (Current): Telegram Mini App Terminal, Simulated Trading, TON Connect V2 integration.
  • Phase 2: Live Info Oracles, TON Mainnet Settlement, Alpha Governance.
  • Phase 3: Multi-chain liquidity aggregation, Nexus AI Agents deployment.
